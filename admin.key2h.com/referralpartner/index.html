Awaken the Rebel Within: Discover Periyar's Legacy

Periyar, the father of the Self-Respect Movement, championed equality, rationalism, and the fight against caste oppression. A visionary who uplifted marginalized communities, he tirelessly promoted gender rights, education, and freedom of thought. As a leader of the Dravidian Movement, he celebrated Tamil identity and resisted linguistic domination.

Periyar’s legacy continues to inspire the pursuit of a just and equitable society. 

Step into Periyar World, an iconic destination dedicated to honoring and amplifying the enduring values of Periyar, the visionary leader who advocated for equality, self-respect, and rational thought. At its center stands a breathtaking 95-foot monument, symbolizing the towering influence of Periyar’s ideals and inviting all to reflect on the transformative power of his teachings. This one-of-a-kind space is enriched with thoughtfully curated features to engage visitors. It’s a place where architecture, art, and activism converge to create a legacy that inspires generations to come.

Periyar World proudly occupies an expansive 27-acre site in Siruganur, located just 25 kilometers from Trichy along the Chennai-Trichy National Highway. At its core stands an awe-inspiring 95-foot statue, elevated on a magnificent 60-foot base, reaching a total height of 155 feet from ground level. This towering monument symbolizes the profound influence of Periyar’s rationalist ideals, serving not only as a landmark but also as a powerful testament to the enduring legacy of equality, humanism, and progressive thought. Periyar World is designed to inspire visitors, inviting them to engage with the values that continue to shape a more just and equitable society.
